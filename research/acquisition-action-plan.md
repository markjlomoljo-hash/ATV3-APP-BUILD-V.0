# Acquisition action plan

## Priority actions

1. **First-party protocol:** define one non-diagnostic next-window outcome, observation window, participant/episode keys, consent, deletion propagation, label QC, representation targets and independent holdout. Obtain legal/clinical/ethics approvals where applicable.
2. **DermNet inquiry:** request acne-only counts, sample/data dictionary, capture conditions, labels, subject keys and a contract covering commercial training/testing, hosted inference, derived weights, redistribution, retention/deletion, geography, DPA and audit. Official lead: https://dermnetnz.org/dermatology-image-dataset .
3. **ACNE04 author request:** seek written commercial training/deployment/derivative rights plus consent, de-identification, identity keys and annotation QC. Official source: https://github.com/xpwu95/LDL .
4. **SCIN legal review:** review the custom licence and original consent/purpose; verify acne volume, label schema, contribution grouping and holdout feasibility before acquisition. https://github.com/google-research-datasets/scin .
5. **Controlled structured data:** identify the exact YODA acne trial and assess the GSK STF114546 data dictionary. Submit proposals only if endpoint and commercial derivative rights are compatible. https://clinicalstudydatarequest.com/Posting.aspx?ID=8 and https://yoda.yale.edu/trials-search/ .
6. **Acne-ID watch:** request IPD only after publication plus its stated 12-month delay and committee-approved DUA. https://www.isrctn.com/ISRCTN45540647 .
7. **DINOv2 evaluation record:** after approved images exist, pin standard DINOv2-small revision, artifact URL, SHA-256, Apache notice and conversion/parity evidence. https://github.com/facebookresearch/dinov2 .
8. **MedSigLIP authorization:** owner/entity and counsel must accept HAI-DEF terms before download/use. Restrict to a validated non-diagnostic cloud experiment. https://developers.google.com/health-ai-developer-foundations/terms .

## Required contract questions

Ask explicitly about commercial product use, training and testing, hosted inference, derived weights/model outputs, redistribution, sublicensing, attribution, geography, duration/revocation, retention/deletion, backups, audit, security, incident response, consent scope, personality rights, de-identification, participant keys and publication rights. A dataset is `rights_unverified` until the signed document answers the relevant questions.
